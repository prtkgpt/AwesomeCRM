# Customer Portal Guide

This guide is for **customers** of cleaning companies that use CleanDayCRM. As a customer, you can view your bookings, manage your preferences, pay invoices, and communicate with your cleaning company.

---

## Getting Started

### Creating Your Account

There are two ways to get a customer account:

**Option A: Invited by your cleaning company**
1. Your cleaning company sends you an email invitation.
2. Click the link in the email.
3. Enter your name and create a password.
4. You are logged in automatically.

**Option B: Sign up through the booking page**
1. Visit your cleaning company's booking page (e.g., `cleandaycrm.com/company-name`).
2. Book a service and create an account during the process.

### Logging In

Visit **cleandaycrm.com** and log in with your email and password. You will be taken to your Customer Dashboard.

---

## Customer Dashboard

Your dashboard gives you a quick overview of:

- **Upcoming Bookings** -- your next scheduled cleaning appointments
- **Recent Bookings** -- past appointments with status and details
- **Outstanding Invoices** -- any unpaid balances
- **Your Preferences** -- a summary of your cleaning preferences

---

## My Bookings

View all your cleaning appointments.

### Booking Details
Each booking shows:
- **Date and Time** -- when the cleaning is scheduled
- **Service Type** -- Standard, Deep Clean, or Move-Out
- **Duration** -- estimated length of the appointment
- **Address** -- which of your addresses is being cleaned
- **Assigned Cleaner** -- who is coming (if assigned)
- **Price** -- cost of the service
- **Status** -- Scheduled, Completed, Cancelled, etc.

### Booking History
See a complete history of all past cleanings, including:
- Dates and services performed
- Amounts paid
- Any feedback you submitted

---

## Managing Your Preferences

Customize how your cleaning company services your home. Go to **Preferences** to update:

### Cleaning Preferences
- **Cleaning Sequence** -- which rooms should be cleaned first (e.g., "Start with the kitchen")
- **Focus Areas** -- areas that need extra attention (e.g., "Kitchen counters and bathroom tiles")
- **Areas to Avoid** -- things not to touch (e.g., "Don't move items on my desk")

### Product Preferences
- **Allergies** -- products you are allergic to (e.g., "No bleach")
- **Preferred Products** -- if you supply your own products
- **Scent Preferences** -- scents you like or want to avoid

### Pet Information
- **Pet Handling** -- instructions for your pets (e.g., "Keep dog in backyard")
- **Pet Feeding** -- whether the cleaner should feed pets if the visit is long

### Access & Entry
- **Key Location** -- where to find the spare key
- **Alarm Code** -- your home alarm code (stored securely)
- **Entry Instructions** -- how to enter the home (e.g., "Use the back door")

### Communication
- **Preferred Contact Method** -- SMS, Email, or Phone
- **Notification Preferences** -- how you want to be notified
- **Language Preference** -- your preferred language

### Special Requests
- **Special Instructions** -- anything extra (e.g., "Water the plants in the living room")
- **Temperature** -- preferred thermostat setting during cleaning

## Paying for Services

### Payment Methods
Your cleaning company may offer several payment options:

| Method | How It Works |
|--------|-------------|
| **Credit Card (Stripe)** | You receive a payment link via SMS or email. Click it to pay securely. |
| **Zelle** | Transfer to the company's Zelle email. |
| **Venmo** | Send payment to the company's Venmo username. |
| **CashApp** | Send payment to the company's CashApp. |
| **Cash/Check** | Pay the cleaner directly or leave payment. |

### Auto-Charge
If enabled by your cleaning company and with your consent:
- Your saved credit card is charged automatically after each cleaning is completed and approved.
- You receive a receipt via email/SMS.

### Invoices
View and pay invoices from the **Invoices** section:
- See invoice details (line items, amounts, due date)
- Track payment status (Draft, Sent, Paid, Overdue)
- Pay outstanding invoices through the provided link

---

## Copay & Insurance

If your cleaning services are covered by insurance (e.g., Helper Bee's):

- Your cleaning company handles the insurance billing.
- You are responsible for the **copay** amount.
- Copay discounts may apply based on your arrangement.
- You can pay the copay via any of the supported payment methods.
- Insurance and copay payments are tracked separately on your bookings.

## Leaving Feedback

After each cleaning, you may receive a feedback link via SMS or email.

### How to Leave Feedback
1. Click the **feedback link** in your message.
2. Rate the cleaning from **1 to 5 stars**.
3. Write optional **comments** about the service.
4. Leave an optional **tip** for your cleaner.
5. Submit.

### Tipping
You can tip your cleaner through the feedback form. Tip options may include:
- Credit card (via Stripe)
- Zelle, Venmo, or CashApp
- Cash

### Leaving a Public Review
Your cleaning company may also send you a link to leave a review on:
- **Google Business**
- **Yelp**

Public reviews help your cleaner and the company grow their business.

## Referral Program

Earn credits by referring friends and family.

### How It Works
1. Find your unique **referral code** in your profile or dashboard.
2. Share it with friends who need cleaning services.
3. When they book their first cleaning using your code, **both of you earn credit**.
4. Credits are automatically applied to your next booking.

### Referral Rewards
- **You earn** a credit (e.g., $25) for each successful referral.
- **Your friend earns** a credit (e.g., $25) on their first booking.
- Rewards may vary by company.

### Referral Tiers
The more people you refer, the higher your tier:

| Tier | Referrals | Benefit |
|------|-----------|---------|
| Bronze | 1-4 | Base reward per referral |
| Silver | 5-9 | Enhanced rewards |
| Gold | 10+ | Premium rewards + bonus credits |

### Tracking Your Referrals
View your referral status in the Referrals section:
- Total referrals made
- Credits earned and used
- Current credit balance
- Transaction history

## Online Booking

If your cleaning company has online booking enabled:

1. Visit their booking page (your company will share the link).
2. Select the **service type** and **date/time**.
3. Enter your **property details** (bedrooms, bathrooms, square footage).
4. Review the **estimated price**.
5. Enter your **contact information** and **payment details**.
6. Submit the booking.

Depending on the company's settings:
- **Instant confirmation** -- your booking is confirmed immediately.
- **Pending approval** -- the company reviews and confirms within 24 hours. Your card is pre-authorized but not charged until after the service.

---

## Estimates

Your cleaning company may send you an **estimate** before booking:

1. You receive a link to view the estimate.
2. Review the service details, pricing, and property info.
3. Click **Accept** to confirm and schedule the booking.
4. If you have questions, contact your cleaning company directly.

## Troubleshooting

### I didn't receive my payment link
- Check your SMS messages and spam/junk folder for email.
- Make sure your phone number is correct in your profile.
- Contact your cleaning company to resend.

### I was charged the wrong amount
- Contact your cleaning company directly. They can adjust the booking and issue a refund if needed.

### I need to cancel a booking
- Contact your cleaning company as soon as possible to cancel or reschedule.

### I forgot my password
- Click **Forgot Password** on the login page.
- Enter your email to receive a password reset link.

---

## Need Help?

For account or booking issues, contact your cleaning company directly using the phone number or email they provided. For platform issues, reach out to support@cleandaycrm.com.
